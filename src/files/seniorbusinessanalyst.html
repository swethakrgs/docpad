Senior Business Analyst

Location: New Jersey, United States

Full-time

Betabulls, Inc. seeks Senior Business Analyst for, NJ office to design and implement business strategies and solutions using software development methodologies such as ASAP, SDLC, RUP, Waterfall, iSlLC and Agile.

Job Description:

• Analyze, prioritize, and draft user and business requirements.

• Collaborate with developers to ensure that software applications meet functional requirements and work with product managers and analysts to analyze workflows, assess environmental factors and identify usage/usability requirements.

• Perform cost benefit, SWOT (Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities and Threats), ROI (Return on Investments) and NPV (Net Present Value) analysis.

• Develop and implement technical specifications, build unit test and system test functional enhancements to SAP applications. Generate reports, design and enhance dynamic ABAP Programs.

• Write system requirement specifications and prepare high-level and technical design documents.

• Develop and extract data from SAP R/3 and support SAP full life cycle implementation including blueprinting, configuration, customization, testing, pre go-live, post go-live.

• Provide production support and perform business requirements gathering and gap analysis activities.

• Conduct data mapping exercises and create business use-case models using UML.

• Create end user and procedures manual for post testing and deployment.

• May travel or relocate to various unanticipated sites throughout the United States.

• Must have a Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology Management, Business Administration, Information Systems or related field and 3 years of experience.

To apply,

Please e-mail resume to hr@betabulls.com. EOE
